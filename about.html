Why this site exists and who it's built for

What this site is

metaphysics.it.com is a simple, independent guide to traditional Chinese and Western metaphysical tools. The goal is straightforward: help you explore BaZi, 9 Star Ki, 12 Daily Officers, and East-West horoscope without hype, fortune-telling, or sales pressure.

Who this is for

  • Curious beginners who want a structured place to learn Eastern and Western frameworks
  • Project managers and operators looking for a lightweight weekly planning lens
  • Solo founders, freelancers, and remote workers who want simple timing checks
  • Business travellers who want a quiet planning ritual before a trip
  • Cautious learners who prefer symbolic guidance over prediction-style content

How readings are generated

Every reading is generated locally in your browser. No account, no upload, and no backend processing. Core date logic uses lunar calendar translation compatible with traditional 12 Daily Officer and 9 Star Ki references.

This is for exploration and reflection, not decision-making. Cross-check important dates with calendar tools and customary local sources.

Coverage

Current tools cover BaZi-style element balance, 9 Star Ki profiles, annual charts, 7-day officer planning, and a combined Eastern-Western horoscope overview.
