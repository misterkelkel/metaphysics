How to learn from this page

What is the Eastern Chinese zodiac?

It is a 12-animal cycle based on birth year. Each animal has simple symbolic traits. Think of it as a friendly mirror, not a rigid fortune.

What is the Western sun sign?

Western astrology uses the sun's position at your birthday. It is symbolic and cultural, not scientific. Many people use it as a reflection tool, similar to Chinese metaphysics.

How should I use both together?

Use them like two lenses on the same subject. Compare where they agree and where they differ. The differences are often more useful than the overlap.

Should I make big decisions from this page?

No. Use it to notice patterns and preferences. For important decisions, use verifiable sources and practical judgment.
