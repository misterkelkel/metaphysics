Love Meter · East + West

How to read this playful meter

What does the temperature mean?

It is a fun composite of your Eastern zodiac relationship, your Five-Element blend, and your Western sun-sign match — not a prediction. Treat it as a conversation starter, not a verdict.

How is the East side read?

We compare your birth-year animals (生肖): 六合 is the closest pairing, 三合 is harmonious, 相冲 is friction. Then we check how your animal elements (五行) generate or control each other. The animal is taken from the real lunar year (Chinese New Year boundary), not just the calendar year.

How is the West side read?

We compare your Western sun signs and their elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water). Same or complementary elements read warmer; opposing elements add a little tension.

Does this predict marriage?

No. It is symbolic and cultural. For the actual wedding day, use the Ze Ri (择日) tool to find a personalized auspicious window.
